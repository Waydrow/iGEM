Team

Our team consist of 11 students and two instructors from Ocean University of China. We come from 5 different colleges, majoring in biology, biotechnology, ecology, bioengineering, aquiculture, mathematics and industrial design.

In the processing of iGEM, we are trying to figure out what we could do with synthetic biology. We will never forget the time we fight together.
